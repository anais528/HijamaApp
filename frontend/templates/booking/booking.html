Setup Required

To activate the booking widget, follow these steps:

  1. Create a free account at calendly.com
  2. Set up your event type (e.g., "Hijama Session - 30 min")
  3. Configure your availability hours
  4. Copy your Calendly scheduling link (e.g., https://calendly.com/your-username/hijama-session)
  5. Replace YOUR-USERNAME and YOUR-EVENT-TYPE in the widget code below (line ~280)
  6. Remove this yellow instruction box from the HTML

Before You Book

  • Arrive 10 minutes early for your appointment
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Avoid eating heavy meals 2 hours before
  • Stay hydrated before and after treatment

What to Expect

Your Hijama session typically lasts 30-45 minutes. Our experienced practitioners will guide you through the process and ensure your comfort throughout the treatment. We use sterile equipment and follow all health & safety protocols.
