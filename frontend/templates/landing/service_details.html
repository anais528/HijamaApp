Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hijama painful?
Most clients report minimal discomfort during Hijama. The sensation is often described as a slight pinching feeling during incision, followed by relief as toxins are removed. We work at your comfort level throughout the procedure.
What's included in the infrared heat therapy?
Infrared heat therapy is included in all Hijama packages to warm up the blood and body before treatment. This helps improve circulation, relaxes muscles, and prepares the body for optimal cupping results. The gentle heat penetrates deep into tissues for maximum therapeutic benefit.
How often should I get treatment?
Treatment frequency varies based on individual needs. For general wellness, monthly sessions are recommended. For specific conditions, more frequent sessions may be beneficial initially. We'll create a personalized schedule during your consultation.
Can I combine Hijama with massage therapy?
Yes! Many clients benefit from combining treatments. All our Hijama packages already include therapeutic massage. You can also book a separate massage session for additional relaxation and muscle relief. We can help you create a comprehensive treatment plan.
Are there any side effects?
Minor side effects may include temporary circular marks, slight soreness, or bruising at treatment sites. These typically fade within a few days to a week. Serious complications are rare when performed by trained practitioners with proper hygiene protocols.
Who should avoid these treatments?
Cupping therapy is not recommended for pregnant women, people with bleeding disorders, those on blood thinners, or individuals with skin infections at treatment sites. We conduct thorough consultations to ensure safety for all clients.
